## The Insulae Draconis Cavalry

Horses were an integral part of medieval life. Here in Insulae Draconis we have an active Cavalry who train their steeds in the skills needed of a Warhorse, the Mounted Skill at Arms. These skills include Tilting for Rings, throwing of the javelin, Beheading our Enemies (usually of the cabbage or apple family), Quintain and various others. At an annual competition we compete to find the finest warhorse, who is awarded the title of Equus Bellator! If you have a horse, want to help out or just interested to see what we do, please get in touch.
