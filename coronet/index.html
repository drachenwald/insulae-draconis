About Their Highnesses
Interests and Likes: Armoured combat, fencing, archery, metalwork, leatherwork, sewing Fiber arts, Bardic arts, dancing, dogs and cats, storytelling and dramatic arts, gardening.
At events, Their Highnesses enjoy: Martial activities, evenings around a campfire, heraldic displays, dogs, entertaining conspiracies and intrigues. At events the highnesses enjoy: A laid back schedule, Down time. Live music and singing. Entertainment during feast and a bardic circle. Board games and cards. A quiet room for meeting people. Welcoming children and newcomers into court
Dislikes: His Highness dislikes a lack of shade or seats, interrupting someone who is talking, and other discourteous behaviours. Her highness dislikes modern things on tables and during feasts and around the event space, being rude to other people and groups.
Regarding food
Favoured Foods: His highness enjoys charcuterie and cheese platters, hearty stews and meat dishes, fruits, nuts, mead, beer, cider Her highness will eat most foods presented, and loves fruit and snacks. She is adventurous and enjoys foods from other countries and cultures. (Please make sure she stays hydrated).
Please do Not Feed Their Highnesses: Leeks (mild allergy), shellfish, offal, olives Smoked Islandic meats, especially Smoked Lamb. No very spicy food. Her highness drinks NO alcohol, but she enjoys syrups made by the arts and science members in water.

Matters of Note

Her Highness is very easy to talk to and enjoys meeting and talking to the populous. Please excuse her memory and names. If she looks confused just tell her your name. Don’t be afraid to invite her into your circle to sing or play medieval games (she is not very good at chess).

About the Coronets

The Coronets of Insulae Draconis (Princes, Princesses, or equal titles from other languages) represent the Crown of Drachenwald in the Principality. As such, they should be treated as any other royal couple in the Kingdom. In essence, be courteous and respectful of what they are representing and of the fact that their time often is not their own. Much of each event they attend will be spent officiating, being in meetings, or just listening to people's opinions. Read more about how to behave around the Coronets including at Court.

The Coronets will hold Court at most events at which they are in attendance. They may have announcements for public business but they also are responsible for bestowing Awards on members where the people of Insulae Draconis have recommended that a member should be so honoured. Insulae Draconis has many ways to reward people for hard work, skill and courtesy. You can read more about awards, check whether someone already has an award and then make an award recommendation
